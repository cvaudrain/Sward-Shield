A Bit About Caitlyn...

Hello!

I'm Caitlyn Sward, founder of Sward and Shield LLC. I'm a registered dietitian nutritionist and certified personal trainer with a passion for fitness and food! I believe that exercise and nutrition are two equal lines of defense for our health. The name "Sward and Shield" symbolizes this, like a sword(exercise) and shield(nutrition) to protect us on our adventure...It also demonstrates that I appreciate a good pun!
Running my own personal training and nutrition counseling practice is amazing not just because I get to do what I love, but because I get to help clients from all walks of life live their best, healthiest lives.

...is to get people moving and eating well! As a trainer I design personalized fitness programs to help folks of all skill levels reach their goals. Using the ACE Integrated Fitness Method, I help my clients to set specific, attainable exercise goals. As a dietitian, I strive to foster healthy relationships with food and promote proper nutrition.

Personal training isn't just for elite athletes anymore. No matter your experience level, I can make the perfect program for you. Using the ACE Integrated Fitness Method, I work with my clients to set specific, attainable exercise goals and to reach those goals at an ideal pace.

I believe that food is more than fuel; it's also an important personal enjoyment, expression, and cultural touchstone! I don't sell diets or supplements, and I definitely won't tell you what foods you can't eat. As a dietitian, I embrace a research-driven approach to food that allows for personal choice, and superior health outcomes.

I offer initial consults at no cost, with no commitment. Our first session is an opportunity for me to get to know you and your goals, as well as give you a good idea of what our program could look like.
